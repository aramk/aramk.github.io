--- layout: post title: The Horrors of Using preg_replace tags: - PHP status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' dsq_thread_id: '571196695' --- If you're going to use replacement text that has '$' or '' in it, chances are it is going to be treated as a backreference. Any parentheses you include in your regex will be captured and placed into the replacement text. If you don't want this, you need to escape the '$' as '$' and the '' as '\'.
